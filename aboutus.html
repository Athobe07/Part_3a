GET TO KNOW US

College Transport System is a system founded by Athobe Ntshonga. It helps students around Cape Town to travel from home to school. This system is provided to college students.The purpose of this system is to provide a safe travelling way for college students as the owner was motivated by his OWN situation of travelling that resulted to the development of CTS. Also, by the fact that a lot of students get lost or kidnaped on their way from school shown by the statistics. The mission of the company is to help college students as they are not provided with private college transportation. Therefore, this business aims to fill that void by providing the college transport. The focus of this business is to expand around the world to other students other than in cape town. This could minimize the risks of students travelling to school by taking them home safely. It is mainly targeting students from colleges.
